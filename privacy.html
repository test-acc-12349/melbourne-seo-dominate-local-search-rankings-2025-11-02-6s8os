Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login information. Cookies can be either "persistent" (they remain on your device until you delete them) or "session" (they are deleted when you close your browser).

Types of Cookies We Use

Melbourne SEO uses the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled. They enable core functionality such as security and network management
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often and whether errors occur
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enable the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced functionality
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements relevant to you and your interests
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website using tools like Google Analytics

Managing Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and performance of our website.

To manage cookies, you can:

  • Access your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Use browser extensions that block tracking cookies
  • Opt out of specific cookie types through our cookie consent tool
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies regularly
